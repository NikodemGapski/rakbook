The fractal is based on an equilateral triangle.
There is one parameter: p (0, 1), which represents the ratio between the next iteration triangle edge length and the current one.
p = a' / a.

Each step the following is done:
1. Divide the base of the current triangle into lengths a * p and a * (1 - p).
2. Draw an equilateral triangle to the left of the division.
3. Go to step 1. with the new triangle.
4. Draw an equilateral triangle to the right of the division.
5. Go to step 1. with the new triangle.

Example: p = 0.7
